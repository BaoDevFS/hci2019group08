Things accumulated during the Human-Machine Communication course

1. Interface design ability:

  • Apply classroom theory into interface design.
  • Create a friendly interface, easy to use.
  • Ensure the principles when designing a website.
  • Minimize errors.
  • Website design helps a lot in life.

2. Ability to work in groups:

  • Although the members initially did not acquire mutual agreement, after a period of working, the group became more united and supported each other throughout the project implementation process.
  • Assign work equally to each member (avoiding teamwork or doing nothing).
  • The members themselves discussed, set out the task that is suitable for their ability to accelerate the project.

3. Ability to listen and understand requirements:

  • Listen to the teacher's comments
  • When being commented on the parts that have not been done well, quickly fix them to suit the requirements given by the teacher.
  • When it was commented that part was right, continue to promote strong points.
